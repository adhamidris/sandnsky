Our Story

Sand & Sky Tours

We design soulful journeys across Egypt—merging desert stillness, Nile intimacy, and the treasured hospitality of the communities we call home. Each itinerary is hand-built by our Cairo-based team to balance signature moments with unexpected, quietly luxurious details.

From the rippling dunes of the Great Sand Sea to sunset feluccas along the Nile, Sand & Sky Tours curates experiences that feel both timeless and deeply personal. We collaborate with veteran Egyptologists, desert explorers, and local artisans to ensure every journey is guided with care.

Our boutique approach keeps groups intentionally small, allows for flexible pacing, and ensures that every traveler has the space to connect with Egypt’s landscapes and rituals. We are here to champion meaningful travel that respects the land, honors culture, and leaves a light footprint.
