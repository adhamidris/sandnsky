Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: 25 March 2026

1. If You Cancel

The timing of your written cancellation determines the refund available. All cancellation times are measured against the scheduled start time of your experience (local Egypt time).

  • ≥ 72 hours before the scheduled start time: Full refund.
  • < 72 hours before the scheduled start time: No refund.
  • No-show: No refund.

If your booking includes any third-party items that are expressly marked as non-refundable at checkout or in writing (for example: limited-capacity permits or pre-booked components), those items may not be recoverable once confirmed. We will always disclose this before you complete payment.

How to cancel: Send your cancellation request in writing and include your booking reference.

2. If We Cancel or Significantly Change Your Tour

Should Kaya Tours need to cancel or make a major change that materially affects your experience, you may choose one of the following:

  • Reschedule to a comparable new date (subject to availability).
  • Switch to another Kaya experience and pay/receive any price difference.
  • Full refund of all amounts paid to Kaya Tours.

The above options may be affected by events beyond our reasonable control where force majeure terms apply (see below).

3. Force Majeure

Force majeure covers extraordinary events such as extreme weather, government closures or restrictions, strikes, civil unrest, and public health emergencies. In these situations we prioritize offering a new date or future travel credit so you can still enjoy your experience when conditions improve. If a cash refund is requested, it may depend on what we are able to recover from any third parties involved in delivering your booking.

4. Refund Timing & Method

Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method. We typically initiate refunds within 10 business days; your bank/card issuer may take additional time for the funds to appear.
