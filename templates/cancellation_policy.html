Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: 15 September 2024

1. If You Cancel

The timing of your written cancellation determines the refund available:

  • ≥ 30 days before the trip start: Full refund minus any non-recoverable supplier costs already paid (see below).
  • 14 – 29 days before start: 70% refund, or one complimentary date change if availability allows.
  • 7 – 13 days before start: 50% refund.
  • < 7 days before start or no-show: No refund.

Some services (e.g., special desert permits, exclusive lodges, charter flights) are fully non-refundable once booked. We will flag these items before you pay your deposit.

2. If We Cancel or Significantly Change Your Tour

Should Sand & Sky Tours need to cancel or make a major change that materially affects your experience, you may select one of the following:

  • Move to a comparable new departure date.
  • Switch to another Sand & Sky tour and pay or receive any price difference.
  • Receive a full refund of all amounts paid to us.

The above options exclude events beyond our reasonable control where force-majeure terms apply (see below).

3. Force Majeure

Force majeure covers extraordinary events such as extreme weather, government closures or restrictions, strikes, civil unrest, and public health emergencies. In these situations we prioritize offering a new date or future travel credit so you can still enjoy your experience when conditions improve. Cash refunds depend on what we are able to recover from local suppliers.

4. Refund Timing & Method

Approved refunds are processed within 10 business days to the original payment method. Bank processing times, international transfers, and card issuer policies may affect how quickly funds appear in your account.
