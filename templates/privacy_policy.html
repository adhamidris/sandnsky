Your Data

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 September 2024

1. What We Collect

We collect information in three primary ways:

  • You provide: Name, contact details, nationality (for permits), dietary requirements, booking details, and any messages you send us.
  • Automatic: Site analytics (pages visited, device and browser type, approximate location) and cookies (see our Cookie Policy).
  • Payment: Transactions are handled by our payment partner; we never store full card numbers on Kaya systems.

2. Why We Use It

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • Deliver, manage, and personalize your bookings.
  • Provide customer support and respond to your requests.
  • Meet legal obligations (e.g., permits, invoicing, accounting).
  • Improve our website, itineraries, and services using aggregated analytics.
  • Send trip updates and occasional newsletters or offers (you can opt out anytime).

3. Sharing

We only share your information with trusted partners as needed:

  • Suppliers such as hotels, transport providers, guides, and permit authorities to operate your itinerary.
  • Payment processors, analytics platforms, and email service providers under contractual safeguards.
  • Authorities or regulators when required by applicable law.

4. Retention

Booking records are typically retained for seven (7) years to meet accounting and legal requirements. Marketing data is kept until you unsubscribe or request deletion.

5. Your Choices & Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. You can opt out of marketing communications at any time using the unsubscribe link or by contacting us directly. Email privacy@sandandskytours.com with any privacy requests.

6. Security

We maintain reasonable technical and organizational safeguards and partner with reputable third-party processors. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we continually review our measures to protect your information.
